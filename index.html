unith.ai is a conversational AI platform that helps businesses create interactive and personalized digital experiences. The platform specializes in building AI-driven virtual humans that can engage in real-time conversations, provide customer support, and deliver information in a natural and scalable way.

By combining generative AI, speech recognition, and realistic avatars, Unith.ai enables organizations to transform how they connect with audiences—whether in marketing, customer service, or interactive training.

Key highlights of Unith.ai include:

  • Seamless deployment of conversational AI across web and mobile platforms.
  • Realistic, human-like digital presenters for more engaging communication.
  • Integration with APIs and business systems for dynamic, personalized interactions.
  • Scalable solutions for industries such as education, entertainment, and corporate training.

How to Use This Demo

  • Click “Talk to Digi Scott” in the video panel to begin.
  • If you would like to speak with your voice, click the microphone button once the session has started.
  • You may also type your questions if you prefer text-based interaction.
